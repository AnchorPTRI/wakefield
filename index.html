An open letter to the communities of South County:

Thank you for recognizing Anchor Physical Therapy as your choice for orthopedic and sports rehabilitation. Our patients have had tremendous success and excellent results since we opened our doors in May 2017 providing comprehensive, personalized Physical Therapy for general and complex orthopedic conditions through non-surgical, pre-surgical and post-surgical rehabilitation methods.
In this day and age of physician-owned physical therapy clinics, Anchor Physical Therapy is proud to be an independent, PT-owned practice that espouses evidence-based practices as an integral part of the healthcare team. We believe that as healthcare providers we play an integral role in the musculoskeletal and neuromuscular health and wellness of our patients and our community. Furthermore, we believe that every patient should have the opportunity to make an informed choice regarding their rehabilitation.
Whether it’s joint replacement, ACL reconstruction or rotator cuff repair, Anchor Physical Therapy provides the experience and expertise that you demand to get you back on the field, the court, or the job. We accept most major insurance plans and do not require a physician referral for many of our non-Medicare patients. With almost 15 years of experience in sports medicine and orthopedic rehab, you can trust Anchor Physical Therapy to provide the results you want and the outcomes you need to get back to the best version of you.
It’s your choice. Choose Anchor Physical Therapy.

About Us

Mark A. Torok, MS, PT, MBA
Owner and Founder, Anchor Physical Therapy

Mark has been a practicing licensed Physical Therapist for 12 years. He started his healthcare career as a US Navy Fleet Marine Force Corpsman and served for 6 years prior to enrolling in the University of Rhode Island. He holds a Bachelor of Arts in Biology, a Master of Science in Physical Therapy and a Master of Business Administration from URI. Mark has focused his career primarily in the outpatient orthopedics and sports physical therapy setting and has collaborated with the foremost orthopedic surgeons in the region. For almost a decade, Mark was part of a leading integrated orthopedic/physical therapy practice in southern RI as a senior therapist and Clinic Manager. During his tenure he designed, implemented and managed several successful programs and a satellite clinic for the practice. He then went on to become the Regional Director of Operations for a large physical therapy company with seven outpatient locations throughout the state of Rhode Island overseeing facilities ranging from small ancillary clinics to multi-site practices.

Mark has had extensive experience with orthopedic interventions, surgeries and treatments and has amassed a wealth of knowledge in the non-surgical and post-surgical rehabilitation of these musculoskeletal, neuromuscular and sport-related conditions. As a former baseball player and military veteran, Mark’s clinical interests lie in rehabilitation of the athlete, ACL reconstruction rehab, biomechanics, joint replacement and reconstruction rehab and sport-specific physical therapy. Mark resides in Wakefield, RI with his wife and two daughters and enjoys fitness, music and the beach.

Anchor Physical Therapy is the culmination of Mark’s experience and practice in the field of orthopedic and sports medicine rehabilitation.

Services

Anchor Physical Therapy

Anchor Physical Therapy offers a wide scope of practice within outpatient orthopedic and sports physical therapy. We provide referral and self-referral evaluations along with physical therapy interventions such as manual therapy, therapeutic exercise, modalities and neuromuscular re-education. Anchor Physical Therapy offers comprehensive post-surgical rehabilitation for even the most complicated of orthopedic surgeries including joint replacement and resurfacing, joint reconstructions, trauma, dysfunction and overuse injuries. Anchor Physical Therapy also provides performance evaluations for athletes and functional outcome measurements for all patients.

Rehabilitation and Therapy Programs


Evaluation & treatment of musculoskeletal and neuromuscular conditions will include but are not limited to:

*Non-surgical rehabilitation
*Post-surgical rehabilitation
*General and complicated orthopedic injuries and/or conditions
*Sports-related rehabilitation
*Athlete-specific rehabilitation
*Overuse and repetitive motion injuries and conditions
*Cervical, thoracic, lumbar, sacroiliac and pelvic dysfunction
*Post-trauma, osteoarthritis, post-fracture, sprains and strains
*Physical Therapy interventions including manual therapy, therapeutic exercise, neuromuscular re-education, gait training, balance and vestibular therapies and soft tissue mobilization
*Physical Therapy modalities including electrical stimulations, ultrasound, TENS, spinal traction, ionto/phonophoresis and kinesiotaping.

Wellness Programs

Anchor Physical Therapy will also offer community wellness programs including

*Wellness consultations (posture, nutrition, BMI, fall assessments)
*Workstation and workplace ergonomic assessments
*Running and Functional Athletic Assessments
*Pre-surgical gait training (crutches, stair ambulation, transfers)
